    • Introduction

    The two goals Clean Water and Sanitation and Zero Hunger are connected because hunger and clean water are needs that are very crucial in many developing countries. A developing country is a country with a less developed industrial base and a low Human Development Index(HDI) relative to other countries. Food and clean water are essential needs for all humans and they're both human rights. 1 in 9 people are malnourished and 3 in 10 people do not have access to safe drinking water. The main focus is on how Canada as a developed country can help developing countries that are in need of food and clean water.
